Speciality-Grade Coffee

We are free to choose the best beans from various roasters at any given time, which means that all of our coffees will be outstanding, not just some of them.

Education Emphasis

One of our main aims, as experienced teachers, is to educate the Philadelphia coffee-drinking market in a friendly and home-like environment.

Focus on Taste

We want to improve our customers' lives by letting them taste coffee in the way that it was meant to be prepared and showing them that it can be really sweet without the addition of sugar.

Enrich the Community

We are proud Philadelphians and plan to be active in the community, especially the lovely Bella Vista area.

The Right Accompaniments

Along with our excellent coffee selection, we'll be selling pastries, chocolates and juice that will pair well with our coffees.

Our Story

We started our business in April 2015 in a very simple way: starting our Instagram account. We eventually registered our business as an LLC in September 2015. We started because we traveled to dozens of countries while living in England and ended up seeking out great coffee shops everywhere we went, not just because we are coffee lovers, but because we love everything that a coffee shop can be. We played chess in a tiny basement bookstore/coffee shop in Bergen, Norway. We made a great connection with a small record/coffee shop owner in Amsterdam, Netherlands. We had our Eureka moment in a shop in Bath, UK where we both finally tasted all those flavors that bags of coffee promise but never really seem to deliver.
